About TechCon 2024

History

TechCon is a startup company established in 2021, focusing on bringing together young, talented tech professionals from across Africa to showcase their intelligence, skills, and capabilities.
It also serves as an institution that helps aspiring tech professionals build and advance their careers.,
After three years of operation, TechCon has emerged as the best tech grooming institution across Africa.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to identify, nurture, and showcase the brightest young tech talents across Africa.
We are dedicated to providing a platform that empowers aspiring tech professionals to develop their skills, advance their careers, and make a significant impact in the tech industry.
Through innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning, we strive to be the leading institution for tech talent development in Africa.
